Cucumber Salad


Penny's Cucumber Salad

An extraordinarily refreshing pico de gallo

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro

Cooking Instructions
  1. Chop cucumbers
  2. Chop tomatoes
  3. Chop onion
  4. Chop cilantro
  5. Crush garlic
  6. Mix and serve (for more flavor, leave in fridge overnight)
