Sausage And Rice


Sausage Lorenzo

Grilled kabobs with succulent vegetables and sausage served over rice

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Fresh Vegetables
  • Pineapple
  • Rice

Cooking Instructions
  1. Cook rice
  2. Place vegetables and sausage on kabob
  3. Grill kabobs
  4. Serve kabobs with side of rice
