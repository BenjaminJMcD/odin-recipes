Vincenzo's Spicy Lasagna

This rustic dish is the traditional Italian recipe for lasagna with spices added for an extra kick

Ingredients

  • Fresh Pasta
  • Ground Steak
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Mozzerela

Cooking Instructions
  1. Layer ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 400
  3. Bake for 50 minutes
  4. Cut and serve
